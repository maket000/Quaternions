Quaternion Multiplication

The product of two quaternions is called the Hamilton product and is determined as follows:
For two quaternions, q1 = a1+b1i+c1j+d1k, and q2 = a2+b2i+c2j+d2k,
q1q2 = a1a2 - b1b2 - c1c2 - d1d2
            + (a1b2 + b1a2 + c1d2 - d1c2)i
            + (a1c2 - b1d2 + c1a2 + d1b2)j
            + (a1d2 + b1c2 - c1b2 + d1a2)k
